# Manqoba Data Deletion Instructions

**Last updated: 19 February 2026**

If you have created an account in the Manqoba app using Facebook Login, you may request deletion of your data at any time.

## How to Delete Your Data

### Option 1 — Delete your account inside the app
You can delete your account directly from the MyMuntu app:
1. Open the app  
2. Go to **Profile / Settings**  
3. Select **Delete Account**  
4. Confirm the deletion  

This will permanently remove:
- Your authentication profile  
- Your Firestore user document  
- All associated data  

### Option 2 — Request manual deletion
If you cannot access the app, email us at:

**arthur.rusike@gmail.com**

Include:
- Your full name  
- The email associated with your Facebook account  
- Your Facebook UID (optional)

We will delete your data within 7 days.

---

## Facebook Data Deletion Callback

If you are visiting this page from Facebook’s Data Deletion Request flow, your data will be deleted as described above.
